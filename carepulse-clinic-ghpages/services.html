Cardiology +

We evaluate chest pain, high blood pressure, palpitations, and irregular heartbeat, with ECG and echocardiogram available on the same visit for most cases.

First visit: bring any previous ECG or blood pressure records. Plan for 40–50 minutes if testing is needed.

ECGEchocardiogramStress testBP management
Orthopedics +

Joint pain, fractures, back pain, and sports injuries are assessed with in-house X-ray, with a physiotherapy plan arranged before you leave if needed.

First visit: wear or bring loose clothing that allows the affected joint to be examined easily.

X-ray on-siteFracture carePhysiotherapy referralSports injuries
Pediatrics +

Routine vaccinations, growth and development tracking, and urgent care for fevers, infections, and injuries in children from birth through the teenage years.

First visit: bring any existing vaccination card so we can continue the same schedule.

VaccinationsGrowth trackingUrgent child care
Dental Care +

Cleanings, fillings, root canals, extractions, and cosmetic work, in a chair-side setting with numbing options explained before any procedure begins.

First visit: a routine check and cleaning typically takes 30 minutes.

CleaningsRoot canalCosmetic dentistry
Ophthalmology +

Vision testing, cataract screening, and treatment for eye infections, with glasses prescriptions fitted on-site the same day where possible.

First visit: if you wear glasses or contacts, bring your current prescription.

Vision testingCataract screeningGlasses fitting
General Medicine +

Fevers, infections, diabetes management, and routine health check-ups, with lab work processed in-house so most results are ready the same afternoon.

First visit: come in fasting if you'd like blood sugar or cholesterol checked the same day.

Health check-upsDiabetes careSame-day labs
