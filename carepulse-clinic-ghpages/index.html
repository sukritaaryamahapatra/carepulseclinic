How a visit works

Three steps, start to finish.

01

Book your slot

Choose a department and time online, or call the front desk directly. You'll get a confirmation with your token number.

02

See the specialist

Walk in near your slot time. Your history is already on file, so consultations start with your concern, not paperwork.

03

Get your plan

Leave with a written treatment plan, any prescriptions, and a follow-up date already on the calendar if you need one.
