About CarePulse

Built by doctors who got tired of referrals.

CarePulse opened in 2011 because three founding doctors kept sending patients across town for basic tests. We put the departments patients need most often under one roof instead.

Our story

From one consultation room to six departments.

CarePulse began as a single general medicine practice run by Dr. Nalin Verma. When patients kept asking where to go next for a cardiology opinion or a child's vaccination, the answer became: here.

Over 13 years, that grew into a proper multi-specialty clinic — cardiology, orthopedics, pediatrics, dental, ophthalmology, and general medicine, all sharing one patient record so no one repeats their history at every door.

We still keep the thing that started it: a doctor who has time to listen before reaching for a prescription pad.
